Sunshine
We keep what the government deletes.
Sunshine is a public, append-only mirror of government procurement records — an unofficial archive, not a portal. Data model: OCDS. Scrapers extend switchr24/mcp-india-tenders. Sibling of SansadSaar.

AI settings

Sunshine summarises tenders either on your device (WebGPU — no key, no server, weights cached after a one-time download) or via your own API key (sent straight to the provider, never to our infrastructure — VaultMind pattern).
